## 🧬 Who am I?

I'm **Leander** — an AI/ML engineer who genuinely enjoys making machines smarter (and occasionally making them do my job for me 😄).

I started off crunching data, fell down the deep-learning rabbit hole, and now I spend my days building agents, training models, and occasionally wondering if I've created something that'll replace me.

When I'm not pushing commits, you'll find me reverse-engineering how stuff works, obsessing over clean pipelines, or reading yet another paper I "totally have time for."

## 🎯 What I'm chasing

- 🤖 **Autonomous AI agents** that actually work end-to-end (no hand-holding)
- 🏥 **AI in Healthcare** — making diagnostics smarter with multimodal ML
- 🛠️ Ship side projects fast, iterate faster
- 📖 Contribute to open-source AI tooling
- 🌍 Eventually build something that makes a real dent
